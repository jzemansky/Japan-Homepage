Tokyo Plans

Jackson Hole

Jackson Hole is a famous burger joint in Tokyo that has been featured in shows such as NANA. We plan to visit and get the Jackson Burger!

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower was built in the 50s and was the tallest building in Tokyo up until 2012. It is a radio and observation tower, and is reminiscent of the Eiffel Tower. Hopefully it'll be a sunny day when we go to the observation deck at the top!

Meiji Shrine

This shrine is famously located in the heart of Tokyo, covering 70 hectares of forest surrounded by the dense urban city. It was established in 1920, to commemorate the virtue of Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken who took the initiative to make a foundation of modernized Japan.

Shibuya Scramble

The Shibuya Scramble makes up a multitude of street crossings in the Shibuya ward of Tokyo. The Scramble occurs when police stop all traffic to allow people to cross, and since all traffic halts people walk straight across a wide expanse of roadways.
