Osaka Plans

Kitatake Udon

Kitatake Udon is a noodle shop in Osaka that holds a dear place in my heart. While finishing my degree in college, I would watch videos filmed in this restaurant of how they prepare and cook their foods. We are excited to go and try several of their menu items!

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is famous for it's views, design, and history. It has foundations extending into the river beneath, and was historic in unifying the regions of Japan in the 16th century.

Dotonbori

Osaka's most famous tourist destination, this entertainment district is filled with neon lights, shops, restaurants, and bars. The street food in Dotonbori is purported to be some of the best in the world.

Don Quijote

Don Quijote is a famous trinket and discount store in Japan. This store offers a wide variety of items such as toys, costumes, clothes, food items, and more.
